Vanilla Cake Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 3/4 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 tsp vanilla extraact
  • 1/2 cup milk

Other Items Needed

  1. whisk
  2. bowl
  3. pan
  4. measuring utensils

    5. Steps

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9x9 inch pan.
    2. In a bowl, cream together sugar and butter. Beat in the eggs slowly and then add in the vanilla extract. Combine flour and baking powder in a seperate bowl and mix the wet ingredients wiht the dry ingredients. Finally mix in the milk until the batter is smooth. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
    3. Bake for 30-40 minutes in the preheated oven.
    4. Enjoy :)
